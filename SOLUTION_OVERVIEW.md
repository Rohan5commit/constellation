# Solution Overview

## Product Vision

Constellation is a web-based rediscovery system for recovering the meaning of forgotten digital fragments before that meaning disappears.

Instead of behaving like a notes app or productivity dashboard, the product acts like a thoughtful layer between storage and memory. It helps users collect fragments, cluster them into constellations, and resurface them with context, care, and credible next steps.

## What the Product Does

### 1. Capture

Users can add fragments as:

- text notes
- screenshots
- links
- voice thoughts
- memory prompts
- person-based reminders

### 2. Group

An AI-assisted clustering layer gathers fragments into constellations such as:

- career ideas
- people to reconnect with
- trip plans that stalled
- things I meant to learn
- small moments I do not want to lose

### 3. Resurface

The rediscovery feed brings back fragments based on:

- unfinished intent
- emotional weight
- repeated appearance across different contexts
- timing and recency gaps
- person or project relevance

### 4. Reconstruct Meaning

Each fragment detail view answers a simple question: **Why is this back now?**

The product reconnects a fragment to time, people, projects, mood, and other saved traces so users can make an informed choice.

### 5. Help Users Decide

For every resurfaced fragment, the user can:

- act on it
- reconnect with it
- archive it
- let it go

## Why This Is Distinct

Constellation is not trying to become another all-purpose life organizer. Its novelty comes from its emotional and interaction model:

- it values rediscovery over raw capture
- it treats context as the missing layer
- it uses AI to suggest possibilities, not fabricate certainty
- it makes forgotten fragments feel legible, not overwhelming

## NVIDIA NIM Role

NVIDIA NIM is used where it directly improves the experience:

- **Clustering Agent** groups fragments into constellations
- **Context Recovery Agent** writes short, careful explanations of why a fragment may matter
- **Reflection Agent** drafts gentle resurfacing prompts
- **Reconnection Agent** suggests low-pressure next steps for people-related fragments

All outputs are explicitly framed with uncertainty, using language such as “might relate to” and “seems connected to”.

## MVP Scope

The MVP prototype focuses on the clearest emotional through-line for judging:

- storytelling landing page
- onboarding
- rediscovery feed
- constellation view
- fragment detail view
- why resurfaced explanation
- reconnect flow
- weekly reflection
- privacy page
- seeded demo mode

## Emotional Payoff

The product does not just help users keep things.

It helps them **find what still matters**.
