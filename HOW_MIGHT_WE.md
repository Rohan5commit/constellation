# How Might We

- How might we help people recover the meaning of saved fragments, not just the fragments themselves?
- How might we make Lost & Found feel emotionally immediate from the very first screen?
- How might we group scattered digital traces into patterns that feel insightful, not arbitrary?
- How might we resurface forgotten items in ways that feel gentle, timely, and believable?
- How might we help users understand **why** something matters now without overclaiming certainty?
- How might we support reconnection with people and projects without turning reflection into pressure?
- How might we create a visually memorable prototype that still feels calm, premium, and clear?
