# Submission Summary

**Title**  
Constellation

**Problem Summary**  
People rarely lose digital fragments because they are deleted. They lose them because meaning gets scattered across screenshots, tabs, notes, voice memos, reminders, and unfinished drafts until the original intention disappears.

**Solution Summary**  
Constellation is an AI-assisted rediscovery system that helps users gather forgotten fragments into meaningful constellations, understand why those fragments mattered, and decide whether to act, reconnect, archive, or let go.

**Design Process Summary**  
The concept was framed around fragmentation, context loss, and emotional overload. The process prioritizes research visibility, opportunity mapping, clear user flows, prototype-driven storytelling, and fast validation artifacts that can be completed with real interviews before judging.
