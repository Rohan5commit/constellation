# Jobs To Be Done

**Status:** Provisional JTBD framing. Replace or revise after real interviews.

## Functional Jobs

- When I save something that feels important, help me find it again without remembering exactly where it lives.
- When fragments belong together, help me see the pattern.
- When something resurfaces, help me understand why it matters now.
- When a fragment points to a person, project, or unfinished plan, help me choose a low-pressure next step.

## Emotional Jobs

- Help me feel less like my life is scattered across disconnected apps.
- Help me recover intention, not just data.
- Help me revisit things with curiosity and clarity instead of guilt.
- Help me feel that my saved fragments still belong to a coherent self.

## Pains

- digital clutter makes saved items feel unreachable
- context disappears faster than content
- meaningful fragments get buried next to trivial ones
- reminders work for tasks, but not for emotional or half-formed intentions
- too much resurfacing would feel invasive or creepy

## Desired Outcomes

- resurfaced fragments feel timely and believable
- grouped fragments create an “aha” moment
- users quickly understand what they can do with a found fragment
- the system feels reflective rather than extractive
